Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Diamant Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications available on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

Our apps may collect the following types of information:

  • Analytics data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as app opens, session duration, screen views, and crash reports. This helps us improve app performance and user experience.
  • Advertising data: Our apps may display ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Crash reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash and error data to help us identify and fix issues.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address. Our apps do not require account creation or login.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google)
  • Google AdMob

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of collected data.

Ad Personalization

Our apps may show personalized ads based on your interests using data collected by Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such data.

Data Retention

Analytics and crash data collected through Firebase is retained according to Google's standard data retention policies. We do not store any personal data on our own servers.

Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, most data processing is handled by our third-party providers. You can exercise your rights through your device settings or by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
